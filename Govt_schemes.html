Government Schemes for Tree Protection

National Afforestation Programme

Type of Aid: Financial Grants

Monetary Amount/Range: ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per hectare

Compensatory Afforestation Fund Act

Type of Aid: Monetary Compensation

Monetary Amount/Range: ₹1,00,000 - ₹2,00,000 per hectare

Green India Mission

Type of Aid: Project Funding

Monetary Amount/Range: ₹50,000 - ₹1,00,000 per hectare

Pradhan Mantri Udyogita Vikas Abhiyan

Type of Aid: Subsidies & Loans

Monetary Amount/Range: Up to ₹1,00,000 in subsidies

Forest Rights Act, 2006

Type of Aid: Community Grants

Monetary Amount/Range: ₹50,000 - ₹5,00,000

State-Specific Initiatives

Type of Aid: Varies by State

Monetary Amount/Range: ₹10,000 - ₹1,00,000 per project
